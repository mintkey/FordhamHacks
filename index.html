Our Mission

FordhamHacks is Fordham Universityâ€™s first ever 24-hour hackathon set to be hosted at the Lincoln Center campus (heart of NYC) on January 25-26, 2020. Drawing inspiration from Fordham being a liberal arts school, the FordhamHacks team strives to plan an event that welcomes students from all different backgrounds and skill levels. From programmers, to entrepreneurs, to designers, to scientists, the FordhamHacks team aims to foster innovative problem solving by gathering some of the brightest minds in and around NYC.

About Us

FordhamHacks was started by a team of future programmers, business moguls, and designers that currently attend Fordham University. We saw a need for an on-campus event that would offer students the opportunity to take their individual experiences and wide range of knowledge accumulated from the liberal arts curriculum towards creating solutions for important issues. Our team also thought it would be beneficial to invite students from other schools to have a greater diversity of ideas and potential to connect. Thus, FordhamHacks was born.
